Donate Books to Needy Children!

Are you trying to figure out how you can positively influence a child's life? There's nowhere else to look! Give a child in need the gift of reading by sponsoring a child in our Every Summer Has a Story reading program. The purpose of this program is to provide books and a backpack to underprivileged children in our communities so they can continue to read throughout the summer. In addition to giving a child the tools they need, your assistance will encourage and motivate them to learn.


Up With Books works to stop the "summer slide" by giving kids the fundamental reading skills they need. You can help create a better future for these young learners and support their academic success by becoming involved and sponsoring a child.


Just picture the look of delight on a child's face when they open a backpack full of books and opportunities. A child's life will be transformed by your kindness, and it will also provide them with the opportunity to realize their full potential.


Join us in making a difference by supporting a child, family, group, class, or school!

Donation

If you are interested in making a monetary donation, please follow the "Donate" button below. You will also be able to read about our campaign, and learn about the ways your generous contribution will help us put books in the hands and hearts of children in need.

Publishers and Booksellers

One excellent source of donated new books is the book industry, including publishers, booksellers, and others. Please get in touch with us if you or someone you know who works in this industry would like more information about what we do, how they can help, or would like to make a donation!

Thank you to those who have donated books and money to support our mission.
